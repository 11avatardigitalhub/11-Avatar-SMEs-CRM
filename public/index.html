Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free? +

Yes! Our Free Forever plan includes all core features: unlimited leads, pipeline management, client tracking, revenue monitoring, GST invoices, and WhatsApp integration for up to 5 team members. No credit card required.

Is my data secure? +

Absolutely. We use Google Firebase infrastructure with bank-grade 256-bit encryption. Your data is stored in Indian servers (asia-south1). We are GDPR and IT Act compliant.

Can I use it on mobile? +

Yes! 11 Avatar Digital Hub is a PWA (Progressive Web App). Install it on your Android or iOS device for a native app-like experience. Works offline too!

How is WhatsApp integrated? +

We support the official WhatsApp Business API. Send messages, use templates, manage chats, and send broadcasts — all from within the CRM. One-click WhatsApp from any lead or client record.

Can I import existing data? +

Yes! Import your existing leads, clients, and contacts via CSV or Excel files. We also provide JSON export/import for complete data portability.
