Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how 11 Avatar Digital Hub collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our Service.

1. Introduction

11 Avatar Digital Hub ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of all individuals who use our CRM platform. This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website, web applications, APIs, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. This policy is compliant with:

  • India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act)
  • EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (India)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve the CRM platform.
  • Authentication & Security: Verify identity, detect fraud.
  • Communication: Transactional notifications, support responses.
  • Personalization: Customize experience, remember preferences.
  • Analytics: Analyze usage, monitor performance.

  • Billing: Process payments, generate GST invoices.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share with:

  • Service Providers: Firebase/Google Cloud, Razorpay/Stripe, SendGrid, MSG91, CloudWA.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law.
  • Business Transfers: Merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.
  • With Your Consent: Explicit consent for other purposes.

6. Data Storage & Security

Data is stored on Google Cloud Platform servers in Mumbai, India (asia-south1). Security measures include:

  • Encryption at Rest: AES-256
  • Encryption in Transit: TLS 1.3
  • Access Controls: Multi-tenant isolation, 8-level RBAC
  • Audit Logging: Comprehensive audit trails
  • Regular Backups: Automated daily backups, 30-day retention
  • 2FA Support: Two-factor authentication

7. Data Retention

  • Account Data: While account active + 90 days post-termination.
  • Business Data: As per subscription. Export available 30 days post-termination.
  • Transaction Data: 7 years (Indian tax law requirement).
  • Communication Logs: 1 year for audit purposes.
  • Backups: Daily 30 days, weekly 12 weeks.
