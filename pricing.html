Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! All plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You can explore all features before committing.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you get immediate access. When downgrading, changes apply at the next billing cycle.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely via Razorpay and Stripe.

Do you offer GST invoices?

Yes! All plans include GST-compliant invoices. For Indian businesses, we provide proper GST invoices with applicable tax breakdowns.

Is my data secure?

Your data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We use Google Cloud's Mumbai data center for data residency in India.

Do you offer discounts for startups?

Yes! We offer special startup pricing. Contact us at sales@11avatardigitalhub.cloud with your startup details.
