1. Overview

At 11 Avatar Digital Hub, we strive to ensure complete customer satisfaction. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be issued and subscriptions may be cancelled. This policy is compliant with Indian consumer protection laws and is part of our Terms of Service.

We believe in transparency. All our subscription plans come with clearly defined features and limitations. We encourage you to review plan details and utilize any free trial periods before making a purchase decision.

2. Subscription Refunds

2.1 Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscription payments are non-refundable except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Unavailability: If the Service experiences downtime exceeding 0.5% in a calendar month (less than 99.5% uptime), you are eligible for a prorated credit for the downtime period.
  • Billing Error: If you are incorrectly charged due to a technical error on our part, a full refund of the erroneous charge will be issued within 7 business days.
  • Duplicate Payment: If you are charged twice for the same billing period, the duplicate charge will be refunded in full.

2.2 Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscriptions may be eligible for a prorated refund if cancelled within the first 30 days of the subscription period. The refund amount is calculated as:

Refund = (Annual Fee Paid) - (Monthly Rate × Months Used) - (Processing Fee of 5%)
Where Monthly Rate = Standard monthly price of your plan

After 30 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable but remain active until the end of the prepaid period.

2.3 Free Trial

All new users are eligible for a 14-day free trial of any paid plan. You will not be charged until the trial period ends. You may cancel anytime during the trial without any charges.

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds under any circumstances:

  • Consumed SMS, Email, or WhatsApp messaging credits.
  • Third-party service fees (Meta WhatsApp Business API charges, payment gateway processing fees).
  • Custom development work that has been delivered and approved.
  • Setup, onboarding, or migration services that have been completed.
  • Domain registration or SSL certificate purchases.
  • Late payment fees or penalties.
  • Subscription fees for periods already elapsed (monthly plans).

5. Cancellation Policy

5.1 How to Cancel

You may cancel your subscription at any time through:

  1. Log into your account at 11avatardigitalhub.cloud
  2. Navigate to Settings → Billing & Subscription
  3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Confirm cancellation and provide optional feedback
  5. You will receive a confirmation email with cancellation details

5.2 Effect of Cancellation

  • Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.
  • You retain access to all features until the subscription expires.
  • You may export your data at any time during the active period.
  • After expiry, your account will be downgraded to the Free plan.
  • Data beyond Free plan limits may be archived and eventually deleted per our Data Retention Policy.
  • You may reactivate your subscription within 30 days to restore full access.

Important: Cancelling your subscription does not automatically trigger a refund. You must separately request a refund if eligible per Section 2 of this policy.

6.1 Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, send an email to billing@11avatardigitalhub.cloud with:

  • Your registered email address or account ID
  • Invoice number of the payment in question
  • Reason for the refund request
  • Any supporting documentation (screenshots, error logs)

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Service Disruption

In the event of significant service disruption (more than 24 continuous hours of complete unavailability), affected customers may request a service credit equivalent to the downtime period, calculated on a prorated basis.

7.2 Feature Deprecation

If a core feature that materially affects the functionality of your subscribed plan is permanently removed without a reasonable alternative, you may be eligible for a prorated refund of the remaining subscription period.

7.3 Duplicate or Fraudulent Charges

If you notice any unauthorized or fraudulent charges on your account, contact us immediately at urgent@11avatardigitalhub.cloud. We will investigate and resolve within 48 hours.

7.4 Force Majeure

No refunds shall be provided for service disruptions caused by events beyond our reasonable control, including but not limited to natural disasters, government actions, war, terrorism, internet infrastructure failures, or third-party service outages.
