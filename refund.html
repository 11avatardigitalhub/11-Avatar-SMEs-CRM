1. Overview

ℹ️

At 11 Avatar Digital Hub, we strive to ensure complete customer satisfaction. This policy outlines the terms under which refunds may be issued and subscriptions cancelled. This policy is compliant with Indian consumer protection laws and is part of our Terms of Service.

We believe in transparency. All our subscription plans come with clearly defined features. We encourage you to utilize any free trial periods before making a purchase decision.

2. Subscription Refunds

2.1 Monthly Subscriptions

Monthly subscription payments are non-refundable except in these circumstances:

  • Service Unavailability: If downtime exceeds 0.5% in a calendar month (less than 99.5% uptime), you are eligible for a prorated credit.
  • Billing Error: Incorrect charges due to technical error will be refunded within 7 business days.
  • Duplicate Payment: Duplicate charges refunded in full.

2.2 Annual Subscriptions

Annual subscriptions may be eligible for prorated refund if cancelled within first 30 days. After 30 days, annual subscriptions are non-refundable but remain active until end of prepaid period.

2.3 Free Trial

All new users get a 14-day free trial. No charges until trial ends. Cancel anytime during trial without any charges.

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Consumed SMS, Email, or WhatsApp messaging credits.
  • Third-party service fees (Meta WhatsApp API, payment gateway fees).
  • Custom development work delivered and approved.
  • Completed setup, onboarding, or migration services.
  • Domain registration or SSL certificate purchases.
  • Late payment fees or penalties.
  • Subscription fees for periods already elapsed.

5. Cancellation Policy

5.1 How to Cancel

  1. Log into your account at 11avatardigitalhub.cloud
  2. Navigate to Settings → Billing & Subscription
  3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
  4. Confirm cancellation and provide optional feedback
  5. Receive confirmation email with cancellation details

5.2 Effect of Cancellation

  • Subscription remains active until end of current billing period.
  • Full access retained until subscription expires.
  • Data export available during active period.
  • After expiry, account downgraded to Free plan.
  • Data beyond Free limits may be archived per Data Retention Policy.
⚠️

Important: Cancelling your subscription does not automatically trigger a refund. You must separately request a refund if eligible per Section 2.

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Service Disruption

For significant disruption (>24 continuous hours of unavailability), affected customers may request a service credit for the downtime period.
